Gallery Controls

Slider Controls

Adjust the number of images displayed per row (1-15 columns)

  • - button: Decrease columns
  • + button: Increase columns
  • Drag slider: Real-time adjustment

Keyboard Shortcuts (Image Viewer)

  • ← → Arrow keys: Navigate between images
  • - and = / + : Zoom out and Zoom in
  • J and K : Rotate left and Rotate right
  • 0 : Reset zoom and rotation
  • Esc : Close image viewer

Mouse Controls

  • Click any image to open full view
  • Click ← → buttons to navigate
  • Click to rotate
  • Click to zoom
  • Click ✖ to close viewer
